Why Frida Kahlo was an important person?

Her real name Magdalena Carmen Frida Kahlo Calderón.From a young age she went through many situations of sadness and suffering that caused her immobility in various parts of her body but still dedicated her life to which she was trying to develop, painting. In his paintings I always reflect rural people who suffer in a fantasy environment. Thematics of pain, blood, abortions fear of death and suffering was the most typical of his paintings. It was also a woman committed to the world, she was against capitalism by getting involved in the politics of her country.

Timeline of Frida Kahlo's life

  • 1907 - Birth
  • 1913 - At age 6 she gets polio that affects her spinal cord.
  • 1920 - Starts her career as a painter and also a little in medicine
  • 1925 - She suffers a serious accident on a bus that left her in a very bad state.
  • 1929 - She married Diego Rivera
  • 1930 - She gets pregnant
  • 1932 - Diego Rivera falls in love with Frida Kahlo's sister and they divorce
  • 1933 - She and Diego Rivera returns.
  • 1953 - She is amputated one leg and is in rehab
  • 1954 - One year after her leg is cut, she dies of pulmonary embolism in Coyoacan, Mexico City, Mexico
